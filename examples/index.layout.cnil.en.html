Our tips to create a strong password (in French)
Enter your sentence
For a password of 12 characters or more, your sentence should contain at least:
  • About twelve words
  • A nomber
  • A capital letter
  • A punctuation or a special character (dollar, sharp, ...)

The password

Is paired with your sentence :
  • Warning: your password does not contain a lowercase
  • Warning: your password may contain a date
  • Warning: your password contains a sequence of numbers
  • Warning: Your password contains characters that follow each other in alphabetical order
  • Warning: Your password contains characters that follow each other on the keyboard
  • Warning : Your password contains too many identical characters that follow each other
To remember your password :
  • Memorize your sentence with the capitals, numbers and punctuation marks.
  • Get first letter of each word, then keep numbers and punctuation marks.
> Strengthen your password
Your password strength can be improved by applying the following tips :
  • Don't choose a number between 1950 and 2049
  • Add a punctuation mark or a special character (dollar, sharp, ...)
  • Use a smiley to replace a word, such as replace "smile" by :-)
